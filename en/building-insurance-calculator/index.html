Note: Premiums are average values for German building insurance (Wohngebäudeversicherung) tariffs in 2026. Actual premiums vary by insurer (Allianz, AXA, HUK-COBURG, Gothaer, VHV), deductible, dynamic adjustments and special tariffs. 'Wert 1914' is the sum insured in 1914 gold marks — insurers apply the sliding new-value factor (~21.5 for 2026) to convert it into today's rebuild value. Natural-hazard cover is significantly more expensive in high-risk zones (ZÜRS 3/4) and may require a deductible.
